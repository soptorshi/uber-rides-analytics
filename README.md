# 🚗 Uber Ride SQL Data Analysis

## 📌 Objective
Analyze ride booking data to understand demand patterns, cancellations, pricing, revenue, and customer behavior across vehicle types.

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- Average ride distance is nearly uniform (~24.6 km) across all vehicle types  
- Auto is the most used vehicle type, followed by Go Mini and Go Sedan  
- UPI is the most preferred payment method, followed by Cash  
- Auto has the highest number of "No Driver Found" cases  
- Average fare is similar across vehicle types (~₹500)  
- Fare per km ranges between ₹36–₹38, with Go Sedan highest  
- Customer cancellation rates are similar (~6.7%–7.3%)  
- Driver ratings are consistent (~4.23)  
- Auto generates the highest revenue  
- Evening shows the highest demand period  

---

## 🧠 Business Insights
- Strong preference for low-cost rides (Auto dominance)  
- Similar cancellation rates suggest pricing parity  
- High "No Driver Found" for Auto indicates supply shortage  
- Evening peak highlights commuter-driven demand  
- Consistent ratings show uniform service quality  

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Increase Auto driver supply to reduce unmet demand  
- Improve driver allocation during evening peak hours  
- Promote digital payments for faster ride completion  
- Optimize pricing for lower-demand vehicle types  
- Maintain consistent service quality
